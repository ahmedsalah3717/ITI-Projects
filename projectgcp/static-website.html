DevOps - The Intersection of Development and Operations

DevOps is a methodology that combines software development (Dev) and IT operations (Ops) to shorten the systems development life cycle while delivering features, fixes, and updates frequently in close alignment with business objectives.

Key Components of DevOps

  • Continuous Integration (CI)
  • Continuous Delivery (CD)
  • Infrastructure as Code (IaC)
  • Monitoring and Logging

Benefits of DevOps

  • Increased collaboration and communication between teams
  • Improved speed and quality of software releases
  • Reduced risk of failures and downtime
  • Increased scalability and efficiency of IT infrastructure
